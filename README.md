# Faultline

**One thumb. Hold the line.**

Faultline is a one-thumb reflex arcade game about keeping a system alive while everything
tries to crash it. Faults rain down — tap, hold, and release in rhythm to clear each one
before it slips past the line. Easy to learn, ruthless to master. A run lasts seconds;
"one more" lasts hours.

iPhone · Portrait · iOS 17+ · Plays fully offline · No account required.

---

## How to play

Four clean gestures, all with one thumb:

- **Tap** — clear a quick fault as it reaches the fix line.
- **Double-Tap** — two fast taps to crack a tougher one.
- **Press (charge)** — hold to charge, then release when the ring turns green.
- **Hold & Release** — hold, then release inside the green zone to deploy.

Nail the green zone for a **Perfect**. Miss, and your System Stability takes the hit —
drain it to zero and it's a hard crash.

## What's inside

- **Free modes:** Deadline Rush, Ranked (monthly seasons + global leaderboards), and the
  Daily Challenge (the same run for the whole world, five tries a day).
- **Faultline Pro modes:** Chaos Build and Perfect Fix.
- **Climb the ranks:** Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Master → Faultline
  Grandmaster. Seasons reset monthly.
- **Collect a crew:** 8 free starter avatars, with the full 18+ roster (and mythic
  legends like The Oracle) in Faultline Pro.
- **Boss fights & a Backlog** to discover, rich haptics, and an original soundtrack.

### FAQ

**Do I need an account or internet to play?**
No. Faultline plays fully offline with no sign-up. Game Center is optional and only used
for global leaderboards.

**What does Faultline Pro unlock?**
The Chaos Build and Perfect Fix modes, the full avatar and boss collections, the complete
Backlog, and all future gameplay content — one purchase, lifetime access.

**Is Faultline Pro pay-to-win?**
No. Ranked and the Daily Challenge are free and identical for everyone; Pro gives no
scoring, timing, or leaderboard advantage.

**I bought Pro but it's not showing (new device / reinstall).**
Open the Upgrade screen (the gold "Pro" pill on the home screen, or Settings) and tap
**Restore Purchases**. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple Account.

**How do I turn off Game Center?**
On your device: **Settings → Game Center**.
